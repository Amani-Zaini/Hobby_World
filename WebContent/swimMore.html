Swimming Course

Discribtion:

This class is designed for adult swimmers with little to no experience in the water. We will work to help adults conquer their fear of swimming and introduce basic concepts of stroke technique and water safety. The majority of this class takes place in the shallowend of the pool. Class is 45 minutes long.
